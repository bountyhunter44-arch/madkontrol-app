# Uncertainty Model Concepts

## Purpose

Uncertainty visibility is a core product feature, not a weakness.

## Uncertainty Types

- Data missing.
- Source unverified.
- Forecast uncertainty.
- Financial uncertainty.
- Implementation uncertainty.
- Political feasibility uncertainty.
- Distributional impact uncertainty.
- Legal uncertainty.
- Model interpretation uncertainty.

## Confidence Labels

- `high`: supported by strong, current, relevant evidence.
- `medium`: plausible but dependent on assumptions.
- `low`: speculative or weakly supported.
- `unknown`: insufficient information.

## Required Behavior

AI should say:

- "This is uncertain because..."
- "The answer depends on..."
- "A different assumption would change..."
- "This needs human or expert review..."

AI must not turn uncertainty into false certainty for readability.
