# Citizen Impact Model

## Purpose

Public policy should be explained in terms people can understand.

## Impact Categories

- Household economy.
- Access to services.
- Time and administrative burden.
- Rights and obligations.
- Health and welfare.
- Education and work.
- Local community effects.
- Intergenerational effects.
- Vulnerable group effects.

## Required Questions

- Who benefits directly?
- Who pays directly?
- Who is affected indirectly?
- Who may be overlooked?
- What changes in daily life?
- What is uncertain?
- What alternatives exist?
