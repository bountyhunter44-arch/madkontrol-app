# System Map

## Status

Planning only. No runtime exists.

## Conceptual Layers

### 1. Policy Intake

Future purpose:

- Accept a policy proposal, party program excerpt, budget idea, legislative draft, or public debate question.
- Preserve raw input separately from AI interpretation.
- Label source, date, author, jurisdiction, and uncertainty.

### 2. Policy Analysis

Future purpose:

- Break policy into goals, instruments, funding assumptions, target groups, risks, timelines, and dependencies.
- Distinguish facts, estimates, values, and political priorities.

### 3. Scenario Simulation

Future purpose:

- Explore possible consequences over short, medium, and long time horizons.
- Show who may benefit, who may bear costs, and where uncertainty is high.
- Avoid presenting forecasts as guaranteed outcomes.

### 4. Compromise Engine

Future purpose:

- Detect overlap between actors.
- Map conflicts.
- Generate compromise candidates with tradeoffs.
- Preserve real disagreement instead of forcing false consensus.

### 5. Public Explanation

Future purpose:

- Translate analysis into plain language.
- Show facts versus assessments.
- Explain affected stakeholders and alternative options.
- Make uncertainty visible.

### 6. Governance and Transparency

Future purpose:

- Enforce neutrality principles.
- Log assumptions and uncertainty.
- Publish explanation templates.
- Require human review before high-impact public use.
