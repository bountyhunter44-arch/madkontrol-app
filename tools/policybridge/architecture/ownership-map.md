# Ownership Map

## Current Package Ownership

`tools/policybridge/` owns:

- Foundation documentation.
- Policy and stakeholder registries.
- Governance boundaries.
- AI role definitions.
- Workflow definitions.
- Public explanation templates.
- Conceptual module maps.

## Future Module Ownership

### Policy Intake

Owns raw policy input metadata and source labeling.

Must not own user identity, publishing, or final analysis.

### Analysis Layer

Owns structured breakdown of policy goals, instruments, costs, tradeoffs, risks, and assumptions.

Must not claim final truth or hide uncertainty.

### Scenario Simulation

Owns possible outcome mapping and feasibility checks.

Must not present projections as guaranteed predictions.

### Compromise Engine

Owns overlap detection, conflict mapping, and compromise candidate generation.

Must not force consensus or rank parties secretly.

### Public Explanation Layer

Owns plain-language summaries and public-facing templates.

Must not omit uncertainty, affected groups, or alternative interpretations.

### Governance Layer

Owns neutrality principles, AI role boundaries, and implementation gates.

Must not become an opaque political filter.
